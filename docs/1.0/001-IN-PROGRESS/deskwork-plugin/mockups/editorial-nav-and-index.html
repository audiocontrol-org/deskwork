Editorial Studio

A reference of the dev surfaces — pipeline, review desk, browse, manual. Begin where the work is.

¶ Pipeline i. — 1 surface
  1. I Dashboard /dev/editorial-studio

    Press-check. The calendar across all sites; awaiting press; recent proofs; voice-drift signal.

¶ Review desk ii.–iii. — 2 surfaces
  1. II Shortform reviews /dev/editorial-review-shortform

    Cross-platform copy desk. Reddit, LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram — galley slips, one per platform.

  2. III Longform reviews /dev/editorial-review/<slug>

    Per-entry margin notes, decisions, iterate flow. entry-by-entry Reach via the Dashboard or Content view; each review is opened against a specific slug.

¶ Browse iv.–v. — 2 surfaces
  1. IV Content view /dev/content

    The shape of the work. A drillable tree of nodes; click any to read its head matter and browse its scrapbook.

  2. V Scrapbook /dev/scrapbook/<site>/<path>

    Research, receipts, working notes. Addressed by hierarchical path; secret items appear in their own section. path-addressed Reach via the Content view's per-node drawer, or address directly.

    The workflow, the skill catalogue, the names of the things — read once, return when the work asks.
