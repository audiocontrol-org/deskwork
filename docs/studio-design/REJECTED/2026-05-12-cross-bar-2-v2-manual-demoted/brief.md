# Cross-bar 2 refinement v2 — Manual demoted

## What

Two variants. **Option A:** Drop Manual entirely from nav; the bar's left region shrinks to 2 cells (Desk · Short); Manual moves to a small `?` glyph in the upper-right of every folio masthead. Contextual region grows to 1-4 cells. **Option B:** Keep Manual at slot 3 of the nav region but render it quieter (just `?` glyph, no label, faded color at rest). Same tap target; less visual weight.

## Why rejected

v2's payoff (entry-review keeps its full 4-tab bar including Scrapbook; no compression) was real — but v3 made the next leap: *"What would it look like if, instead of enumerating the nav options, we collapsed them all to a single affordance that opens a list of choices?"* The picker-cell refinement was a step further down the simplification path. v4 then dropped the picker entirely (Desk-as-hub). v2 was a midway stop; v4 retired it.

## When

Documented 2026-05-12; superseded by v3 (collapsed picker) and ultimately by v4 (star nav).
