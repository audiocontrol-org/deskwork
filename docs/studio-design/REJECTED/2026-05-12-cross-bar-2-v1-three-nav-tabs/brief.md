# Cross-bar 2 refinement v1 — three nav tabs

## What

The first refinement pass on the split-bar pattern after operator approved Cross-bar 2 as the direction. The bar's left region carries three fixed nav tabs (Desk · Short · Manual), with the kraft hairline divider separating the right region's 1-3 contextual cells. Cap: 6 total cells (3 nav + 3 contextual). Entry-review longform's 4-tab v0.18 bar (Outline · Notes · Scrapbook · Actions) must compress to 3 by moving Scrapbook into the Actions sheet as a Folio section (the Scrapbook-3 precedent).

## Why rejected

Operator's framing: *"we don't need to give that much affordance to the manual. It won't be used very often."* Manual at 1/3 of the nav region over-billed its operator-frequency (weeks-to-months, vs Desk + Short at daily). Refinement v2 demoted Manual to a masthead `?` glyph; v3 collapsed nav to a single picker cell; v4 dropped nav from the bar entirely. v1's three-tab nav was the starting point but didn't survive the simplification trajectory.

## When

Documented 2026-05-12; superseded by v2 (Manual demotion) and ultimately by v4 (star nav).
