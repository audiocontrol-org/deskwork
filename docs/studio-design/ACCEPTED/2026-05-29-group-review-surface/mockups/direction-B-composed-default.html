Direction B — Composed multi-lane default with list toggle
Default group review surface = miniature multi-lane swimlane view scoped to the group's members. Section-head toggle flips to flat list (Direction A's shape) for operators who prefer linear scan.

  • Toggle state is per-group (sticky in localStorage keyed by entry UUID).
  • Empty-members fallback: the Members section drops; surface shows the group's artifactPath content OR a centered empty-state CTA when there's nothing to render.
