Direction A — Members-as-list with sticky lane-stage summary strip
Default group review surface = flat list of members. Task 7.4's multi-lane composition is reframed as a 1-line sticky summary strip at the top — same data, less chrome, no horizontal scroll on mobile.
  • "Structure over scrolling": the summary strip carries pipeline-shape; the list carries member-detail.
  • No mode-state to track; no toggle to learn.
  • Lane is communicated via the colored left rail on each row + a lane tag in the meta line.
  • Empty-members fallback: the entire Members section drops; the surface degrades to a single-column rendering of the group's artifactPath (or empty-state CTA).
