Lane Bar

Per-lane sub-navigation chip strip within the Longform section. One lane visible at a time + a Combined matrix view.
Proposes standards change Per DESIGN-STANDARDS.md § Studio navigation model: "There is no bar nav region; there is no destination picker; there is no top-level tab bar." The lane bar in this direction is NOT top-level navigation — it's a sub-section content switcher WITHIN the Longform section, switching between lane bodies. If you pick this direction, the standards entry needs an amendment carving out "sub-section sub-navigation MAY use a chip-bar when surfacing N parallel collections within one section, e.g. multiple lanes under Longform." Rationale: the original rule was about avoiding destination pickers that displace the star-nav model; this is intra-section content switching, semantically different.
